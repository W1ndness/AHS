个人详情

一、研究领域或方向

专注于结晶多孔材料（COFs，MOFs）以及低维纳米多孔材料在乏燃料后处理中的应用研究。 相关工作以第一作者发表在Nature nanotechnology，Chemical communications等期刊上。

二、工作经历

2020年-，西安交通大学，特聘研究员

2016年-2020年，莱顿大学，博士后研究员

三、学术成果介绍

1. X. Liu, M. He, D. Calvani, et al. Power generation by reverse electrodialysis in single layer nanoporous membrane made from core-rim polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons. Nature Nanotechnology, 2020, 15, 307-312.

2. X. Liu, W. T. Fu and E. Bouwman. One-step growth of lanthanide metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) films under solvothermal condition for temperature sensing. Chemical Communications. 2016, 52, 6926-6929.

3. X. Liu, S. Akerboom, M. de Jong, et al. Mixed-lanthanoid metal–organic framework for ratiometric cryogenic temperature sensing. Inorganic Chemistry, 2015, 54, 11323-11329.

4. X. Liu, M. A Siegler and E. Bouwman. Ratiometric thermometer based on a lanthanoid coordination polymer. European Journal of Inorganic Chemistry, 2016, 8, 2984-2988.
