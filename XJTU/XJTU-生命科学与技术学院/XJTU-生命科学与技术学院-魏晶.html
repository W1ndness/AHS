Biography

 

Professor Jing Wei 

Jing Wei is currently a professor of School of Life Science and Technology, Xi'an Jiaotong University. He was born in March 1986. He received his Ph. D. in Inorganic Chemistry from Fudan University in 2013 with the supervision of Prof. Zhao Dongyuan and Prof. Deng Yonghui, and  B. S. degree in Chemistry from Fudan University in 2008. Then He worked as a postdoctoral research fellow in Monash University with the supervision of  Prof. Wang Huanting and Prof. Simon George from 2013 to 2016. In October 2016, He joined Xi'an Jiaotong University as a Professor. His research interests include nanoporous materials, adsorption and separation, catalysis and biosensor.
