李慧

作者：统计学院 | 日期：2016-10-23 | 点击量：

﻿

李慧，理学博士，副教授，硕士研究生导师。研究方向为时间序列分析，生物统计，非参数统计等。

 

科研论文：

●Estimation of marginal generalized linear model with subgroup auxiliary information,Jie He, Xiaogang Duan,Shumei Zhang and Hui Li, Communications in Statistics - Theory and Methods, Be accepted.

●Additive hazards model with auxiliary subgroup survival information, Jie He,Hui Li,Shumei Zhang and Xiaogang Duan, Lifetime data analysis,2019.

● 带确定成分的求和模型，李慧、张久玲、乔芳芳，北京师范大学学报（自然科学版）2018年.

● Varying-association copula models for multivariate survival data, Hui Li, Zhiqiang Cao and Guosheng Yin, Scandinavian Journal of Statistics,2018.

●Generalized Method of Moments for Additive Hazards Model with Clustered Dental Survival Data,Hui Li, Xiaogang Duan，Guosheng Yin, Scandinavian Journal of Statistics,2016.

●IF1407合约的长记忆回归模型与投资策略研究，曹志强、李慧、童行伟，北京师范大学学报（自然科学版）2015年.

●基于干预模型的黄金价格分析，曹志强、李慧，数学的实践与认识，2015年.

●Censored quantile regression with varying coefficients，Guosheng Yin， Donglin Zeng and Hui Li, Statistica Sinica,2014.

● Generalized method of moments estimation for linear regression with clustered failure time data. Hui Li and Guosheng Yin, Biometrika,2009 96: 293-306.

● Least squares estimation of varying-coefficient hazard regression with application to breast cancer dose-intensity data. Guosheng Yin and Hui Li(2009), The Canadian Journal of Statistics, Vol. 37, No 4, 659-674.

● Power-Transformed Linear Quantile Regression With Censored Data，Guosheng Yin, Donglin Zeng and Hui Li, Journal of the American Statistical Association, Vol. 103, No. 483,(September 2008), 1214-1224.

● Partially Linear Additive Hazards Regression With Varying  Coefficients，Guosheng Yin, Hui Li,Donglin Zeng and Yong Zhou, Journal of the American Statistical Association, Vol. 103, No. 483. (September 2008),1200-1213.

● Local likelihood with time-varying additive hazards model,Hui Li,Guosheng Yin and Yong Zhou (2007), The Canadian Journal of Statistics, vol. 35, 321-337.

● 粮食价格与居民消费价格关系的统计分析 --1997.1-2005.4年粮价与物价的实证分析，吴泰岳、李慧、张鹏，数学的实践与认识，Vol. 36, No.5, 154-159,2006.

● 多测站测量角量系统误差的最小二乘修正，李慧，刘昭，吴国富，齐全跃，系统仿真学报2005年第5期，第17卷1072-1075.

● An efficient algorithm for the optimal market timing over two stocks，Li Hui and An Hongzhi and Wu Guofu. Acta Math. Appl. Sin. Engl. Ser. 20(2004). No. 3, 411-424.

● 多维时序定阶方法对偏最小二乘回归模型预报精度的改进，崔斌、李慧、吴国富，数学的实践与认识，Vol. 33, No. 8, 72-77, 2003.

● Law of the iterated logarithm of quantile density estimator for left truncated and right censored data，Zhou Yong and Li Hui, 应用概率统计2003 年,第19期,No.1,7-13.

●  舰船运动姿态的多维自回归建模，李慧，吴国富，齐全跃. 系统仿真学报，2003年,第5 期,第15卷.
