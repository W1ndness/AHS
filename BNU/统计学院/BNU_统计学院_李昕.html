李昕

作者：统计学院 | 日期：2016-10-23 | 点击量：

李昕，女，1982年生，教授，博士生导师。2009年于中山大学岭南学院获得世界经济学博士学位，2012年于北京大学国家发展研究院博士后流动站出站，2017年获得“北京市优秀青年人才”荣誉称号，研究方向为世界经济、贸易增加值核算。

七、英文简介

Xin Li is  professor of economics at the Department of Statistics, Beijing Normal University, and research assistant in National School of Development, Peking University. Li’s research focuses on production value-added chain and economic integration. The published papers of Dr. Li are including “A Time Series Database for Global Trade, Production and Consumption Linkage”, “Quantifying the Impact of RMB Appreciation by CGE Model”, etc. and She also wrote as a co-author of the books Road to End Poverty (2008), The Exchange rate, Food Security and Trade War (2010), Income Disparity in China-Crisis within Economic Miracle.
