何元珍

作者：统计学院 | 日期：2016-10-23 | 点击量：

﻿

何元珍，女，1983年生，副教授, 硕士生导师。2011年于北京大学获理学博士，2014年于中国科学院数学与系统科学研究院博士后流动站出站。研究方向为试验设计。

 

一．论文

Cheng, C. S., He, Y., and Tang, B. (2019). Minimal second order saturated designs and their applications to space-filling designs, Statistica Sinica (forthcoming)

He, Y., Lin, C. Devon, and Sun F.S. (2019). Construction of marginally coupled designs by subspace theory, Bernoulli, 25, 2163-2182.

Xiong, S. F., He, X., He, Y. and Mu, W. Y. (2018). Sensitivity analysis using permutations, Statistica Sinica, 28, 817-837.

He, Y., Cheng, C. S., and Tang, B. (2018). Strong orthogonal arrays of strength two plus, The Annals of Statistics, 46, 457-468.

Li, X.D., He, X., He, Y., Zhang, H., Zhang, Z., and Lin, Dennis K. J. (2017). The design and analysis for the icing wind tunnel experiment of a new deicing coating,  Journal of the American Statistical Association, 112, 1417-1429.

He, Y., Lin, C. Devon, Sun F.S. and Lv, B.J. (2017). Marginally coupled designs for two-level qualitative factors, Journal of Statistical Planning and Inference, 187, 103-108.

He, Y., Lin, C. Devon, and Sun F.S. (2017). On construction of marginally coupled designs, Statistica Sinica, 27, 665-683.

He, Y. and Tang, B. (2014).  A characterization of strong orthogonal arrays of strength three, The Annals of Statistics, 42, 1347-1360.

He, Y. and Tang, B. (2013).  Strong orthogonal arrays and associated Latin hypercubes for computer experiments, Biometrika, 100, 254-260.

Ai, M., He, Y., and Liu, S. (2012).  Some new classes of orthogonal Latin hypercubes, Journal of Statistical Planning and Inference142, 2809-2818.

He, Y. and Ai, M. (2012).  Complementary design theory for sliced equidistance designs, Statistics & Probability Letters, 82, 542-547.

He, Y. and Ai, M. (2011).  A new class of Latin hypercube designs with high

dimensional hidden projective uniformity, Frontiers of Mathematics in China, 6,

1085–1093.

国家自然科学基金青年基金，“正交表在计算机试验设计和二阶饱和设计中的深入应用研究”，11701033,  2018.01-2020.12.
