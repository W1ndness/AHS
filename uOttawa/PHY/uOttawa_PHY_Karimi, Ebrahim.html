Biography

Selected publications:

  • H Larocque, I Kaminer, V Grillo, R W Boyd & E Karimi, Twisting neutrons may reveal their internal structure, Nature Physics 14, 1–2 (2018).
  • V Grillo, T R Harvey, F Venturi, J S Pierce, R Balboni, F Bouchard, G C Gazzadi, S Frabboni, A H Tavabi, Z-A Li, R E Dunin-Borkowski, R W Boyd, B J McMorran, and E Karimi, Observation of nanoscale magnetic fields using twisted electron beams, Nature Communications 8, 689 (2017).
  • A. Sit, F. Bouchard, R. Fickler, J. Gagnon-Bischoff, H. Larocque, K. Heshami, D. Elser, C. Peuntinger, K. Gunthner, B. Heim, C. Marquardt, G. Leuchs, R.W. Boyd, and E. Karimi, High-Dimensional intra-city quantum cryptography with structured photons, Optica 4, 1006 (2017).
  • F Bouchard, R Fickler, R W Boyd, and E Karimi, High-dimensional quantum cloning and applications to quantum hacking, Science Advances 3, e1601915  (2017).
  • E. Karimi, and R. W. Boyd, Classical entanglement?, Science 350, 1172 (2015).
  • T. Bauer, P. Banzer, E. Karimi, S. Orlov, A. Rubano, L. Marrucci, E. Santamato, R. W. Boyd, and G Leuchs, Observation of optical polarization Möbius strips, Science 347, 964 (2015).
