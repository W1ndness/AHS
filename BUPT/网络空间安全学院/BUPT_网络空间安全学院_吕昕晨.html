吕昕晨

发布日期：2021-03-30　　 　　点击量：

姓名

吕昕晨

性别

男

学术兼职

IEEE Globecom 2021 TPC member

老师类型

教学科研型

所属中心

软件安全中心

职称

特聘副研究员

承担课程

计算机组成与系统结构

研究方向

移动网络安全、边缘智能

个人介绍

特聘副研究员，硕/博导，主要从事边缘计算、移动网络安全相关研究，发表高水平论文20余篇。

承担课题

主持国家自然科学基金青年基金项目，以项目骨干身份参与国家重点研发计划、国家自然基金专项项目、北京市自然基金多项。

学术成果

近年来，在IEEE JSAC、IEEE TWC、IEEE TMC、IEEE TCOM、IEEE IoTJ等国际权威期刊发表高水平论文20余篇，其中包括中科院1区论文10余篇、ESI高被引论文2篇，部分代表性论文如下：

1. Xinchen Lyu, Hui Tian, Li Jiang, Alexey Vinel, Sabita Maharjan, Stein Gjessing, Yan Zhang. Selective Offloading in Mobile Edge Computing for the Green Internet of Things, IEEE Network, 2018, 32(1): 54-60（ESI高被引）

2. Xinchen Lyu, Hui Tian, Cigdem Sengul, Ping Zhang. Multiuser Joint Task Offloading and Resource Optimization in Proximate Clouds, IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, 2017, 66(4): 3435-3447（ESI高被引）

3. Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu, Y. Jay Guo. Multi-Timescale Decentralized Online Orchestration of Software-Defined Networks, IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2018, 36(12): 2716-2730

4. Xinchen Lyu, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu, Xin Wang, Georgios B. Giannakis, Arogyaswami Paulraj. Optimal Schedule of Mobile Edge Computing for Internet of Things Using Partial Information, IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2017, 35(11): 2606-2615

5. Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu, Eryk Dutkiewicz, Optimal Online Data Partitioning for Geo-Distributed Machine Learning in Edge of Wireless Networks, IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2019, 37(10): 2393-2406

6. Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu. Distributed Optimization of Collaborative Regions in Large-Scale Inhomogeneous Fog Computing, IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2018, 36(3): 574-586

7. Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu, Cooperative Computing Anytime, Anywhere: Ubiquitous Fog Services, IEEE Wireless Communications, 2020, 27(1): 162-169

8. Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Qimei Cui, Ren Ping Liu, Online Learning of Optimal Proactive Schedule Based on Outdated Knowledge for Energy Harvesting Powered Internet-of-Things, IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2021, 20(2): 1248-1262

9. Xinchen Lyu, Chenshan Ren, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu, Xiaofeng Tao. Distributed Online Learning of Cooperative Caching in Edge Cloud, IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, 2020, DOI: 10.1109/TMC.2020.2983924

10. Xinchen Lyu, Wei Ni, Hui Tian, Ren Ping Liu, Xin Wang, Georgios B. Giannakis, Arogyaswami Paulraj. Distributed Online Optimization of Fog Computing for Selfish Devices with Out-of-Date Information, IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2018, 17(11): 7704-7717
