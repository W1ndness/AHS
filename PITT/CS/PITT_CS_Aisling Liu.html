Aisling Liu

  • Peer Tutor
  • Pronouns: she, her, hers

Courses supported: CS-0007, CS-0401, CS-0445, CS-0449, CS-1501, CS-1550, CS-1555, LIS-2030, CMPINF-0401

Major: Master of Information Science

Minor: Human-centered Computing track

Academic Level: First-year master's student

Internship, Research and Experiential Learning: Working with Dr. Konstantinos Pelechrinis to build models in R for NFT and cryptocurrency market analysis.

Post graduation plans: Aiming to get a phD in computer software related field

Why did you become a TA? I could refresh and make my computer knowledge more solid through helping others to learn.

What is the best advice you would give to a student who may need additional support? If you need help, never hesitate to seek resources. Set your mindset to problem-solving mode.
