姓名：任炬

职称：副教授（博士生导师）

邮箱：renju@tsinghua.edu.cn

个人主页：https://juren1987.github.io

教育背景

工学学士（计算机科学与技术），中南大学，中国，2009；

工学硕士（计算机科学与技术），中南大学，中国，2012；

工学博士（计算机科学与技术），中南大学，中国，2016.

教育背景

物联网，网络计算，边缘智能，操作系统

研究概况

长期从事物联网、网络计算、边缘智能及操作系统等方面研究工作。在IEEE/ACM JSAC, ToN, TMC, TPDS, CSUR等国际期刊及ACM CCS, Ubicomp, MobiHoc, SenSys及IEEE INFOCOM等国际会议上发表论文90余篇（CCF A类论文20余篇，ESI高被引论文13篇）。主持承担了国家重点研发计划课题、国家自然科学基金等多项国家级项目。曾荣获国家自然科学基金优秀青年基金、湖南省杰出青年基金，入选中国科协第四届青年人才托举工程、湖南省湖湘青年英才计划，获得IEEE ICC’19及IoP’18国际会议最佳论文奖，IEEE HPCC’19国际会议杰出论文奖，IEEE TCSC青年科学家奖等荣誉。现担任《IEEE Trans. Vehicular Technology》和《Peer-to-Peer Networking and Applications》编委。入选2020年科睿唯安全球高被引科学家及斯坦福大学发布的2019全球前2%科学家榜单。

学术成果

1. J. Gong, X. Zhang, J. Ren*, and Y. Zhang. “The Invisible Shadow: How Security Cameras Leak Private Activities,” ACM CCS 2021, accepted to appear, 2021.

2. J. Gong, X. Zhang, K. Lin, J. Ren*, and Y. Zhang. “RF Vital Sign Sensing Under Free Body Movement,” ACM IMWUT/Ubicomp 2021, virtual conference, Sept. 21-26, 2021.

3. J. Gong, X. Zhang, Y. Huang, J. Ren*, and Y. Zhang. “Robust Inertial Motion Tracking Through Deep Sensor Fusion Across Smart Earbuds and Smartphone,” ACM IMWUT/Ubicomp 2021, virtual conference, Sept. 21-26, 2021.

4. Y. Sheng, J. Ren*, J. Xin, S. Lin and J. Zhang. “Inexact-ADMM Based Federated Meta-Learning for Fast and Continual Edge Learning,” ACM MobiHoc 2021, virtual conference, July 26-29, 2021.

5. D. Wang, J. Ren*, Z. Wang, X. Pang, Y. Zhang and X. S. Shen. “Privacy-preserving Streaming Truth Discovery in Crowdsourcing with Differential Privacy,” IEEE Trans. on Mobile Computing, accepted to appear, doi: 10.1109/TMC.2021.3062775, 2021.

6. F. Lyu, J. Ren*, N. Cheng, P. Yang, M. Li, Y. Zhang and X. S. Shen. “LEAD: Large-Scale Edge Cache Deployment Based on Spatio-Temporal WiFi Traffic Statistics,” IEEE Trans. on Mobile Computing, vol. 20, no. 8, pp. 2607-2633, 2021.

7. Y. Zhang, X. Lan, J. Ren* and L. Cai. “Efficient Computing Resource Sharing for Mobile Edge-Cloud Computing Networks,” IEEE/ACM Trans. on Networking, vol. 28, no. 3, pp. 1227-1240, 2020.

8. F. Lyu, J. Ren*, P. Yang, N. Cheng, Y. Zhang and X. S. Shen. “SoSA: Socializing Static APs for Edge Resource Pooling in Large-Scale WiFi System,” IEEE INFOCOM 2020, virtual conference, July 6-9, 2020.

9. Z. Wang, W. Liu, X. Pang, J. Ren*, Z. Liu and Y. Chen. “Towards Pattern-aware Privacy-preserving Real-time Data Collection,” IEEE INFOCOM 2020, virtual conference, July 6-9, 2020.

10. J. Ren, D. Zhang, S. He, Y. Zhang and T. Li. “A Survey on End-Edge-Cloud Orchestrated Network Computing Paradigms,” ACM Computing Surveys, vol. 52, no. 6, article no. 125, 2019.
