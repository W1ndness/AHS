    Ian Hunter

    George N. Hatsopoulos Professor in Thermodynamics

    Interests

    1. Micro-instrumentation, micro-fabrication, micro-robotics, medical devices.
    2. Conducting polymers, artificial muscle fibers.
    3. Laser imaging systems and instrumentation physics.
