简 介

武汉大学哲学学士（2000-2004）、硕士（2004-2006），德国慕尼黑大学哲学博士（2008-2016），现为武汉大学哲学学院哲学系中国哲学教研室副教授。主要从事德国哲学（主要是现象学和黑格尔哲学）、佛教哲学（唯识学和禅宗）和中国“道”文化的研究。

Jianjun Liis a lecturer in philosophy at Wuhan University. He received his PhD (2016) from the University of Munich (Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München) and spent the periods for MA (2006) and BA (2004) at Wuhan University. He is interested in German philosophy (especially Hegel, Husserl and Bernhard Waldenfels), Buddhism (Yogācāra & Chan/Zen) and the philosophical discourses on Dao/Tao in the Chinese context.
