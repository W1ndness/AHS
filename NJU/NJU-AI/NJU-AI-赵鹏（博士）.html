About Me

I am an assistant professor in School of Artificial Intelligence at Nanjing University, and I am also a member of LAMDA Group. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University in December 2021, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhi-Hua Zhou.

Research Interests

My research interests include topics in machine learning and data mining, especially on their theoretical foundations.
Most recently, I am interested in

  • Machine Learning in Non-stationary Environments
  • Online Learning and Decision Making
  • Learning theory and Stochastic Optimization
