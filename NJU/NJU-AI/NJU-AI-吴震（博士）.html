About Me ([GitHub] [Google Scholar]

Now I am an Associate Researcher at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University (NJU), and a member of NJUNLP group, led by Prof. Jiajun Chen. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology of Nanjing University (NJU) in 2021, supervised by Prof. Xinyu Dai, and B.Sc.degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NUST) in 2016.

Research Interest

My recent research interest mainly includes sentiment analysis, opinion mining, sentiment generation, transfer learning, and deep learning.
