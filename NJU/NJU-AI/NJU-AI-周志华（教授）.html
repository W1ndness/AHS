Research Interest

Research is for curiosity and fun, though practical implications can emerge.

I am interested in general machine learning and data mining technologies and theoretical underpinnings. During the past decades I have been working on the following topics:

  • Ensemble learning
  • Evolutionary learning
  • Imbalanced learning (incl. cost-sensitive learning, class-imbalance learning)
  • Multi-label learning
  • Weakly supervised learning (incl. semi-supervised learning, active learning, multi-instance learning, crowdsourcing learning, learning with noisy labels, etc.)
  • Learning theory
  • Face recognition
  • ......

Some of the above topics are still in my concern, some faded out, while some new ones come in:

  • Open-environment machine learning
  • Machine learning + logic reasoning
  • Learnware
  • ......
